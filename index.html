About

I'm a Chemical Engineering student preparing for a career in industry — oil & gas, pharma, or manufacturing. My coursework gives me a rigorous technical foundation, but I've always believed that the engineers who stand out are the ones who can think, write, and communicate across different domains.

My side projects reflect that belief. I write a weekly Substack essay to sharpen my thinking and communication. I'm building language learning video games in Godot to develop coding skills while exploring how people acquire new languages. I'm also writing a novel and producing educational YouTube content on Spanish and Arabic grammar.

These aren't distractions from engineering — they're evidence that I can learn hard things independently, ship creative work consistently, and communicate complex ideas clearly.
